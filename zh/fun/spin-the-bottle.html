    Frequently asked questions

    How do you play Spin the Bottle online?

    Add at least two player names in the right-side panel, choose a mode (Truth, Dare or Truth-or-Dare), and click SPIN. The bottle will rotate and land on one player. That player must answer the truth prompt, do the dare, or pick between the two depending on the mode you chose.

    Can I customize the prompts?

    Yes. The Truth, Dare and player list are all editable text areas. Type your own prompts — one per line — and they will be picked at random when the bottle lands. The defaults are PG-13 prompts suitable for mixed groups of adults and older teens.

    Is the spin fair?

    Each spin uses your browser's randomness to choose both the final angle and the number of full rotations, so every player has an equal chance on every spin. The bottle never lands between two names — the pointer always points to a single player.

    Can I save my player list?

    Yes. Your player list, mode choice and prompt customizations are auto-saved in your browser on the same device between sessions. Clearing your browser data will reset them.
