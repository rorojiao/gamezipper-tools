Five Facial Features — The Five Mountains

👀 Eyes (The Observer)

Large bright eyes: smart and lively. Narrow eyes: thoughtful. Double eyelids: emotionally rich.

👃 Nose (The Judge)

High bridge: confident and independent. Round tip: good fortune. Wide nostrils: strong financial sense.

👄 Mouth (The Speaker)

Large mouth: generous. Small mouth: detail-oriented. Thick lips: values emotions. Thin lips: eloquent.

👂 Ears (The Listener)

Large ears close to head: smart and long-lived. Thick earlobes: blessed. Ears above eyebrows: high intelligence.
