💡 Troubleshooting Tips

  • If you see "Permission denied", click the camera icon in your browser's address bar and allow access
  • Close other apps that may be using the camera (Zoom, Skype, Discord, OBS, etc.)
  • Check that your camera isn't covered and that the lens is clean
  • On Windows, verify the camera is enabled in Settings → Privacy → Camera
  • Try a different browser if the issue persists
